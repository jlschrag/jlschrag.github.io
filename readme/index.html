Greetings potential employers!

Hiring is expensive, on both sides. So, in the interest of efficiency, I've put together a list of differentiating factors to help determine fit:

  • Have a messy, ambiguous problem that nobody wants to touch? I am interested. Has it been tried & failed before? Even better.
  • I cannot relocate without leaving my kids, so I only consider fully remote positions with companies who can employ in New Mexico, USA.
  • I excel in roles that blend strategy, cross-functional collaboration, & technical execution. Purely heads-down roles I find less enjoyable.
  • When working, I am highly productive. That said, I'm a single dad, so if more than 45 hrs/wk or 10% travel are needed consistently, it is not a fit.
  • Does the company do social and/or environmental good? You get bonus points. :)
  • I evaluate strategically, so I don't pursue roles if:
    • There are internal candidates competing for the role.
    • I was not a referral, and there are referral candidates in the running. (8-18x disadvantage)
    • The role is onsite/hybrid preferred. It just doesn't make sense to start at a disadvantage.
  • Is the org structure one of high trust & autonomy? Sign me up!

Thank you for your understanding! If after reading this you feel the role could be a fit, let's talk!

I do consider fractional roles & contract work.
